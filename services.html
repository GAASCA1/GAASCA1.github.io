SERVICE OFFERED BY THE ORGANISATION

Accommodation and health care services
  1. Assistive Living Units accommodation for the homeless older persons.
  2. Daily and on time well balanced meals.
  3. Provision of transport to health institutions like, clinics and hospitals for their health check-ups as
    per the prescribed dates by the health institutions
  4. Provision and access to Primary Health Care, Social Services and implement Club Activity and
    Healthy Lifestyle to older and older persons with disabilities.
  5. Protection of the Rights and Dignity of Older Persons in terms of the Older Persons Act.
  6. Free transportation to and from the Centre.
Entertainment and Spiritual upliftment services
  1. Spiritual upliftment sessions.
  2. Golden games practices.
  3. Handwork, beadwork and sewing.
  4. Daily active ageing activities. (exercises).
  5. Adult Basic Education and Training classes.
